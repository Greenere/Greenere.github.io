Paper reviewed (11)

Adversarial Attack & Defense

  • Jiawei Su, Danilo Vasconcellos Vargas, Sakurai Kouichi. One pixel attack for fooling deep neural networks. CVPR 2017. arXiv:1710.08864

    One-pixel attack demonstrating the surprising vulnerability.

  • Chuan Guo, Jared S. Frank, Kilian Q. Weinberger. Low Frequency Adversarial Perturbation. UAI 2019. arXiv:1809.08758

    Low frequency restriction for better black-box attacks.

  • Yash Sharma, Gavin Weiguang Ding, Marcus Brubaker. On the Effectiveness of Low Frequency Perturbations. IJCAI 2019. arXiv:1903.00073

    Complete evaluation of low frequency perturbations.

  • Lukas Schott, Jonas Rauber, Matthias Bethge, Wieland Brendel. Towards the first adversarially robust neural network model on MNIST. arXiv preprint 2018. arXiv:1805.09190

    Use generative Bayesian classifiers for robust classfication.

Adversarial Learning

  • Shibani Santurkar, Dimitris Tsipras, Brandon Tran, Andrew Ilyas, Logan Engstrom, Aleksander Madry. Image Synthesis with a Single (Robust) Classifier. arXiv preprint 2019 arXiv:1906.09453

    Re-purpose adversarially trained model for generative applications.

  • Logan Engstrom, Andrew Ilyas, Shibani Santurkar, Dimitris Tsipras, Brandon Tran, Aleksander Madry. Adversarial Robustness as a Prior for Learned Representations. arXiv preprint 2019. arXiv:1906.00945

    Demonstrate the generalizable representations learned by robust classifier.

  • Jörn-Henrik Jacobsen, Jens Behrmann, Richard Zemel, Matthias Bethge. Excessive Invariance Causes Adversarial Vulnerability. ICLR 2019. arXiv:1811.00401

    Propose an complementary adversarial example caused by excessive invariance rather than excessive sensitivity.

  • Hadi Salman, Andrew Ilyas, Logan Engstrom, Ashish Kapoor, Aleksander Madry. Do Adversarially Robust ImageNet Models Transfer Better? arXiv preprint 2020. arXiv:2007.08489

    Demonstrate the improved transferability of adversarially trained classifier.

  • Ali Shafahi, W. Ronny Huang, Christoph Studer, Soheil Feizi, Tom Goldstein. Are adversarial examples inevitable? ICLR 2019. arXiv:1809.02104

    Theoretical analysis for the existence of adversarial example.

  • Zeyuan Allen-Zhu, Yuanzhi Li. Feature Purification: How Adversarial Training Performs Robust Deep Learning. arXiv preprint 2020. arXiv:2005.10190

    Theoretical explanation for the vulnerability of standard training and the learning process of adversarial training.

Invertible Neural Networks

  • Jens Behrmann, Will Grathwohl, Ricky T. Q. Chen, David Duvenaud, Jörn-Henrik Jacobsen. Invertible Residual Networks. ICML 2019. arXiv:1811.00995

    Make the residual network invertible by spectral normalization and weight scaling.
