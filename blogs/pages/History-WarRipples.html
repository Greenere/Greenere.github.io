The Ripples of Wars

By LI Haoyang 2020.10.18

The precise date is ambiguous and these information is not accuarate. This is just a timeline for the question of how many years there are when the world witnessed no war. For the purpose of simplicity, the time is cut in 1500.

In conclusion, it's very hard to find a single year where everywhere in the world is in peace, therefore, the peace is not time-invariant or space-invariant.

Every regime goes into wars right after its establishment and stabilization from rebellions. The land regimes strive for expansion and the ocean regimes strive for colonization, the whole war history of world since 1500 is a history of the expansion and conflicts of different empires companied with rebellions and revolutions of nations.

Everywhere there were ripples of wars, each laid a thread for the initiation of another.
